Why Mental Health Consultation is Important:

Mental health is a vital aspect of our well-being, but often neglected or ignored. Many people suffer from stress, anxiety, depression, or other mental disorders that affect their quality of life and happiness. However, they may not seek help due to stigma, lack of awareness, or access to affordable and convenient services.

Why We Do Not Ask for It:

Despite the importance of medical consultation, many individuals hesitate to seek help due to various reasons. Stigma surrounding mental health issues, lack of awareness about available resources, and concerns about accessibility and affordability are some common barriers that prevent people from reaching out for support.
